EmoTra-TTS Demo Page

Audio samples for the paper: "EmoTra-TTS: Smooth Intra-Utterance Emotion Transitions for Speech Synthesis", submitted to ARR 2026 March.

Abstract

We propose EmoTra-TTS, the first TTS system that enables controllable smooth intra-utterance emotion transitions via continuous VAD conditioning and direction–magnitude decoupled injection.

System Comparison

Comparing our proposed method against baseline systems. All samples are synthesized from the same text input. [Important] All texts are NOT included in the training set.
